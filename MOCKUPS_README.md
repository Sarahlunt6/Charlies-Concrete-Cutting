## ğŸ¨ Mockup Overview

### Mockup 1: "Industrial Heritage"
**Design Direction:** Authentic, hardworking, established vintage aesthetic that honors the logo's sign-painting roots.

**Key Features:**
- Warm parchment color palette (#FDF5E6)
- Script font (Yellowtail) for hero headlines
- Decorative flourish dividers between sections
- Rust orange CTAs with rounded corners
- Testimonials section with quote styling
- Texture overlays for vintage feel

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Yellowtail (script)
- Subheadings: Bebas Neue (bold sans-serif)
- Body: Lora (serif)

**Best For:** Clients seeking a warm, trustworthy, established brand image with personality.

---

### Mockup 2: "Modern Grunt"
**Design Direction:** Clean, high-contrast, precision-focused modern construction aesthetic with heavy-duty industrial feel.

**Key Features:**
- Concrete grey background (#E5E5E5)
- Sharp, geometric layouts with zero border radius
- Sawtooth border patterns
- Golden ochre stats section
- Split hero layout (60/40)
- Strong drop shadows on cards

**Typography:**
- All headings: Archivo Black
- Body: Roboto
- Navigation: Montserrat

**Best For:** Clients wanting a bold, modern, no-nonsense professional image.

---

### Mockup 3: "Blueprint & Precision"
**Design Direction:** Clean, airy, technical expertise focus with architectural precision and dependability emphasis.

**Key Features:**
- Pure white background
- Thin brown border accents
- Blueprint-style grid background
- Ghost button CTAs (transparent with borders)
- Process timeline with numbered steps
- Generous white space throughout
- Technical line drawings as decoration

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Playfair Display (serif)
- Subheadings & Body: Oswald (narrow sans-serif)

**Best For:** Clients emphasizing precision, expertise, and professional sophistication.
